ANIME EXPO 2017

FUNIMATION ANNOUNCES ITS SUMMER SIMULDUB LINEUP

In a pair of posts on Twitter, Funimation relayed its simuldub lineup, which includes 18if, A Centaur's Life, Classroom of the Elite, Restaurant to Another World, Hina Logic: From Luck & Logic, Knight's & Magic and Saiyuki Reload Blast. 


Additionally, the company will stream the dub of Lupin III: Part IV on FunimationNow starting today, July 2 at 1pm PT/4pm ET. New episodes will follow on the same day and time each week thereafter.
